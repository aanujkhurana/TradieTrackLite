## Short Description

No-login job tracking for tradies, stored on your phone.

## Full Description

TradieTrack Lite is a simple job tracker for tradies who need to keep work moving without account setup, cloud sync, or a required backend.

Create jobs on-site, add customer details, record the job address, save notes, track status, attach photos, set local reminders, and export job reports from your device. Your job records, reminders, photos, reports, and backups are stored locally by default and only leave the device when you choose to share or export them.

The app is free to use with banner ads in non-critical areas. A one-time ad-free purchase removes ads and can be restored from the same app store account.

## Key Features

- Start tracking jobs without login or account setup.
- Store jobs, photos, reminders, and reports on the device by default.
- Track pending, in-progress, and completed jobs.
- Search by job title, customer, address, or notes.
- Attach local job-site photos.
- Set local reminders for follow-ups.
- Export job reports and manual backups through the device share sheet.
- Use the free ad-supported version or buy ad-free once.

TradieTrack Lite does not require an account. Normal job workflows do not require a backend, cloud storage, or internet connection. Ads and the one-time ad-free purchase require production AdMob, RevenueCat, and store product configuration in release builds.
