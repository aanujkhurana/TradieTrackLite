# Screenshot Guidance

No final store screenshots are included in this repository yet. Capture screenshots from a real release candidate or store-equivalent simulator build so they accurately reflect the app, ad placement, icon, splash screen, permissions, and store configuration.

## Required Scenes

- Jobs list with a few realistic sample jobs, including pending, in-progress, and completed statuses.
- Empty jobs list to show first-launch simplicity.
- Create job screen with job title, customer details, address, notes, status, and reminder fields visible.
- Job detail screen with local photos, time logged, reminder state, and report/export actions.
- Ad-free purchase screen showing the one-time purchase and Restore Purchase option.
- Backup/export state showing the local data ownership message.

## Capture Rules

- Use fictional customer names, phone numbers, addresses, notes, and photos.
- Do not show real customer data, real job sites, store credentials, RevenueCat identifiers beyond public product IDs, or device notifications containing private information.
- Include both iPhone and Android phone screenshots if submitting to both stores.
- If tablets are enabled later, capture tablet screenshots before enabling tablet availability.
- Capture from production-like builds with production app IDs and store products configured, or clearly mark screenshots as draft-only.

- "Switchboard check" for "Lee Electrical" at "8 Market Lane".
