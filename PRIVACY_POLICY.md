# TradieTrack Lite Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

TradieTrack Lite is a local-first mobile job tracker for tradies. The app is designed to work without an account, required backend, cloud storage, or sync.

## Information You Add To The App

You may enter job records, customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, job addresses, notes, reminders, photos, and report details. This information is stored on your device by default.

TradieTrack Lite does not send normal job records, customer details, reminders, photos, or reports to a TradieTrack backend for the MVP. Data leaves your device only when you choose to export, share, back up, or report it using your device's sharing tools.

## Photos, Reports, And Backups

Photos are stored in app-controlled local storage on your device. Reports and backups are generated locally and shared only when you choose to share them.

Deleting the app may delete app data, including job records and stored photos. You should export backups regularly and keep important records somewhere safe.

## Ads

The free version may show banner ads through Google AdMob. Ad requests are configured to request non-personalized ads only where supported by the ads SDK. Google may still process device, ad, and diagnostic information needed to deliver, secure, and measure ads according to Google's policies.

## One-Time Ad-Free Purchase

TradieTrack Lite may use RevenueCat and the relevant app store to validate and restore the one-time ad-free purchase. Purchase validation does not require a TradieTrack account. The app stores the ad-free entitlement locally after validation.

## Analytics

TradieTrack Lite does not currently include product analytics. If analytics are added later, they should be anonymous, minimal, and limited to information needed to keep the app reliable and improve the product.

## Backend, Accounts, And Cloud Sync

The MVP does not require login, backend storage, cloud sync, teams, or subscriptions. Legacy backend code in the repository is not part of normal app use.

## Your Choices

You can keep using the app without creating an account. You can export job backups and reports through your device share sheet. You can delete app data by deleting the app or using device storage controls, subject to your operating system's behavior.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app publisher through the support contact listed in the app store listing.
