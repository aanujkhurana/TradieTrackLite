# TradieTrack Lite — Refund Policy

_Last updated: 5 June 2026_

TradieTrack Lite offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads.
This document explains how refunds work on each platform.

## Summary

- The purchase is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) on iOS or
  Google (Play Store) on Android. TradieTrack does not see or store
  your payment information.
- Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google, not by TradieTrack.
- The refund window and rules differ slightly between platforms;
  see below.

## Apple App Store (iOS)

- Apple lets you request a refund for an in-app purchase from
  <https://reportaproblem.apple.com>.
- Apple's standard policy and any local consumer-protection laws
  apply. Apple decides whether to grant the refund.
- TradieTrack does not have the ability to override an Apple
  decision.
- If the App does not behave as described in the App Store listing
  and you have not been able to resolve the issue with us first,
  you may have additional rights under your local consumer
  protection law. Please contact us at <support@tradietrack.app>
  before requesting a refund so we can try to help.

## Google Play (Android)

- Google lets you request a refund for an in-app purchase from
  <https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637>.
- For most purchases you have up to 48 hours to request a refund
  through the Play Store. Google decides whether to grant the
  refund.
- TradieTrack does not have the ability to override a Google
  decision.

## EU and UK consumers

- The European Union and the United Kingdom grant consumers a
  14-day "right of withdrawal" for digital content that has not
  been fully delivered.
- The "Ad-free" purchase is delivered immediately (the ads stop
  appearing as soon as the purchase completes). By completing the
  purchase, you ask us to start delivering the content
  immediately. In most jurisdictions, this means the 14-day
  right of withdrawal is reduced or waived once delivery has
  begun. The exact rules depend on your local law and on the
  platform's policy.
- We will honour reasonable refund requests where required by
  applicable law.

## Defects and "not as described"

If the App is defective, crashes repeatedly, or does not behave as
described in the App Store listing, please contact us first at
<support@tradietrack.app>. We will try to fix the issue quickly. If
we cannot resolve it, you can request a refund through Apple or
Google as described above, citing "app does not work as described".
